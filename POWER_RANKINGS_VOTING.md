# POWER RANKINGS VOTING SYSTEM  

This document outlines the instructions for a head-to-head voting system designed for ranking 30 soccer teams. Please follow the steps below to participate in the voting process.  

## Instructions:  
1. **Match Pairings**: Each team will be matched against another for voting. Ensure that there are pairings for all teams.  
2. **Voting Process**:  Each participant will vote for their preferred team in each pairing. Votes can be submitted through a form or directly in the comments of this document.  
   - For example:  
     - Match 1: Team A vs Team B  
     - Vote for your choice: **Team A**  

3. **Vote Counting**: Once all votes are submitted, each pairing's votes will be tallied. Tally the votes for each team across the pairings.  
4. **Rankings**: After votes are counted, teams will be ranked based on the total votes received. The team with the highest votes will be ranked first, and so forth.  
5. **Feedback**: Participants are encouraged to provide feedback on the voting process after each round to improve future rankings.
